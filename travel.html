Check if my country is eligible for an e-visa

If your country is not listed among the eligible countries, please visit the Egyptian Embassy in your country to apply for a tourist visa and provide the required documents upon request.

Cost of Egyptian Visa, as per Feb 2019, is $25. Passport validity is 6 months. For questions and requests, please email fab15@fabfoundation.com

Basic Itinerary

Below is the basic itinerary for Fab15 travelers; tours are not included in the below itinerary.

- July 27: Arrive at Cairo - recommeded hotel Le Meridien Cairo Airport

- July 28: Take a bus/flight to ElGouna

- July 29: Beginning of Fab15 conferences

- August 2: Fab Conference and travel back to Cairo (check recommended hotels here)

- August 3: Fab Festival and Fab15 Closing Ceremony

- August 4: Fab lab tours
